Chicken Teriyaki

Description

Food from japanese origin. It was also the first dish I learned to make

This is going to be my second paragraph of content

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Olive oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Honey
  • Brown sugar
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sesame Oil
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  1. Heat oil in a large non-stick skillet or wok
  2. Add chicken and let cook until slightly brown on bottom and then flip and cook through
  3. Whisk together the teriyaki sauce while the chicken is cooking
  4. Pour sauce into the skillet once the chicken is nearly cooked through and let the sauce cook and simmer until thickened
  5. Serve warm over brown or white rice with steamed vegetables.
